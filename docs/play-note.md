# Play Note

## What does this block do?
This block plays a specific musical note from your chosen scale and lets you add cool sound effects!

## How does it work?
You just give this block a number (like `0` for the first note, `1` for the second note, and so on). It will automatically figure out the correct pitch based on the scale you set up in the `set music config` block. 
You can also choose an effect:
- **Flat:** Plays a steady, straight sound.
- **Beautiful Vibrato:** Adds a wavy, singing effect to the note.
Also, if you tilt your micro:bit forward, the pitch goes down slightly. If you tilt it backward, the pitch goes up!

## Why would you use it?
You can use this block inside a `forever` loop to turn your micro:bit into a musical instrument. By changing the number (for example, using the 'roll' angle of the micro:bit), you can play different notes and make up your own songs!
